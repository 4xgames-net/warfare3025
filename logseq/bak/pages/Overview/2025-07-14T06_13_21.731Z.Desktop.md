## Gameplay
collapsed:: true
	- All units are automated and never directly commanded by the player
	- Unit type/ role  determines default behaviour
	- Orders may alter behaviour
- ## Initiative
  collapsed:: true
	- Actions lets Units gain tokens ("#ticks") - the unit with the fewest goes next
	-

	- Tank
	- Superheavy Tank
	- Close Air Support

- ## Tiers and Scale
	- ### Tier 0
		- Player characters in a TTRPG
	- ### Tier 1
		- Infantry Soldiers
	- ### Tier 2
		- Light Vehicles, Exo Armor ("Terminators")
	- ### Tier 3
		- Tanks
		- Infantry Squad (5 - 10) on single Base
	- ### Tier 4
		- Superheavy Tanks
		- Mechs
		- Exo- / light Vehicle Squad (5) on single base
		- Infantry Platoon (30-50) on single Base
	- ### Tier 5
		- Tank Platoon (3-5) on single Base
